Where Are 4 Bridge Rectifiers Located.

the use of 4 bridge rectifiers in basic electronic household idems is very common since american
wall outlets are AC and lots of electronic household items need DC to run properly.

Here are some electronic household idems that plug into AC american wall outlets and have rectifers in them:

  • Laptop or phone chargers.
  • A electronic piano.
  • A desktop computer power supply.
  • a charging brick.
  • electronic blenders.
  • hair dryers.
  • desktop computer monitors
