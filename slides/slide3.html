What is a Diode.

To understand the four bridge rectifier you need to learn how Diodes work.

A diode has two pins (kinda like a resistor). One pin is called the Cathode and one pin is called the Anode.

In a circuit the Diode acts like a conductor or a closed switch when electrons flow from
the Anode to the Cathode, this lets the current pass through the Diode. But when the Diode
is reversed and the electrons flow from the Cathode to the Anode, the Diode acts like a open
switch or a insulator, this stops the current from passing through the diode.

____________________________________________________________________________________

The vertical line on a diode schematic is represented by a vertical line painted on diodes in real life.
